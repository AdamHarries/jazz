Stompin' at Summerhall

Stompin' Swingin' Good Times.

"Stompin' At Summerhall is one of Edinburgh's newest jazz bands, delivering hot swinging music played by dancers, for dancers.

The band, which consists of Murray Wham - Keys, Siggy Watt - Drums, Fraser Hume - Trumpet & Vocals, Adam Harries - Sax (& some special guests!) are all swing dancers that discovered the joy of dancing to jazz music after years of playing it. Although they love to dance, playing jazz was their first love, and so Stompin' At Summerhall is getting these dancers up on the bandstand together to share their love of the music with the rest of the Edinburgh dance community!

Being swing dancers their playing is informed by their love of dance and so they're sure to serve up a platter of incredible music to keep the dancers stompin' all night long!"
